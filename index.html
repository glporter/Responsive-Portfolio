About Me

I am a Computer Science major at University of North Carolina, Charlotte, in my senior year. I am employed with Wells Fargo as a e-business Consultant/Technical Team Lead. I am originally from Florida. I moved to Charlotte in 1982 to work for IBM. I am an associate Minister and youth worker at Ebenezer Baptist Church in Charlotte. I am a Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM) with the Wells Fargo CIC Toastmasters Club #7358. I love volunteering and facilitate a Youth Leadership Program every spring and a 'One Hour of Code' event in the fall during Computer Science Education Week.
